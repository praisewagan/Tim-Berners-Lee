About Tim Berners Lee

Tim Berners Lee is the founding father of the WWW also known as the World Wide Web. He was born in June 8 1955 in London. He was won many awards such as the Turing Award (2016) Queen Elizabeth Prize (2013) Foreign Associate of the National Academy of Sciences (2009) Order of Merit (2007) ACM Software System Award (1995) He is now He is a professorial research fellow at the University of Oxford and a professor emeritus at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). And most of all, we thank sir Tim Berners Lee, for creating the World Wide Web.
